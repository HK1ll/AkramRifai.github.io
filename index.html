File 02 About

A clear-headed approach to messy security problems

I'm a Cybersecurity graduate of Al-Zaytoonah University of Jordan with three internships under my belt, each spent doing the unglamorous work that actually keeps systems safe: scanning for vulnerabilities, reading logs nobody wants to read, and writing reports a non-technical manager can act on.

My background spans penetration testing, secure Active Directory administration, and lightweight automation with Bash and Python. I'm equally comfortable explaining a finding to a developer over a pull request as I am explaining risk to a stakeholder who just wants to know if the company is exposed.

Career objective

  • Move from internship-level pentesting into a full-time Cybersecurity Engineer role.
  • Apply technical and communication skills to help organizations harden their security posture.
  • Keep building toward deeper specialization in offensive security and digital forensics.

Key strengths

Problem-Solving

Breaks down complex systems to find the root cause, not just the symptom.

Attention to Detail

Catches the small misconfiguration before it becomes the big incident.

Adaptability

Comfortable moving between dev, IT operations, and security tooling.
