File 02 About

A clear-headed approach

I'm a Cybersecurity graduate of Al-Zaytoonah University of Jordan, currently deepening my expertise through a Master's program in Cybersecurity at Zarqa University. My academic work runs in parallel with real-world experience from three internships — each one adding a different layer: offensive testing, automation, and secure system administration.
